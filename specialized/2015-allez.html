S-Works Allez

MSRP: $7,000

With an S-Works level frame, Smartweld technology and SL Hydroforming, full Shimano Dura Ace drivetrain, Roval CLX wheelset, and full monocoque Tarmac carbon fork, the S-Works Allez is ready for any road race or crit. It'll also make your toughest weekend rides your fastest weekend rides.

Allez Expert

MSRP: $2,400

The Allez Expert features a Smartweld frame with SL Hydroforming and full monocoque Tarmac carbon fork. You'll make easy work of the toughest climbs, steepest descents and stay with the fastest group rides with the Shimano Ultegra 11-speed drivetrain (and your impressive determination).
